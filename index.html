About VeriFIRE

Edge AI · QNX · Hardware

VeriFIRE is an edge-AI verification layer designed to work alongside existing smoke alarms.

It combines smoke and humidity readings with rate-of-change patterns to distinguish likely cooking or steam events from developing fire-like conditions. All inference runs locally on the device, so the system remains responsive without depending on the cloud.

VeriFIRE does not replace or silence a certified smoke alarm. It adds context, graded alerts, and sensor-health monitoring while preserving the original alarm as the primary safety layer.
